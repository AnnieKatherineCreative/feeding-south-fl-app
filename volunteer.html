Volunteer

Feeding South Florida distributes millions of pounds of food to approximately 1 million individuals facing hunger and food insecurity.

Each year, more than 40,000 individuals, families, and groups share their time and talents to assist with our hunger relief efforts throughout South Florida.

Each volunteer experience is unique and fulfilling for our volunteers, and makes a tremendous difference in the lives of the families we serve.

Feeding South Florida offers a number of volunteer opportunities for individuals or groups. We offer unique team-building and special event sort days.

Ages 15 years and older may sign up for regular shifts.

Ages 12-14 may volunteer when registered with an adult ages 18 and older.

There are special shifts the 2nd Saturday of each month for ages 6 and older.
