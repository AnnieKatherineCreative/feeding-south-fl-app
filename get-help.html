Get Help

We are here to help you! Feeding South Florida provides direct-service programs to individuals in need of assistance. This includes benefits application assistance, access to our choice pantry, referrals of longer-term food assistance at a partner agency, and a home delivery if you need immediate help.

FAQ

Where are your Client Services Centers located?
Main Warehouse: 2501 SW 32 Terrace Pembroke Park, FL 33023 Feeding Palm Beach County: 4925 Park Ridge Blvd Boynton Beach, FL 33426
Can I get food immediately?
Yes. You can order a Food Box to be delivered if you need immediate assistance. Click "Order a Food Box!"
When are you open?
Our Client Services Centers are open Monday – Friday, 8:30 a.m. – 11:30 a.m. and 1:30 p.m. – 4:30 p.m. by appointment only.
Can I talk to someone who can help?
Either e-mail clientservices@feedingsouthflorida.org, chat with us online, or call 561.659.5070 in Palm Beach County, or 954.518.1857 in Broward & Miami‑Dade Counties.
